Thank you for using Anno!

Here are a list of commands you can use to have me take care of your annoucments!

-?schedule: Adds an announcment to the queue
-?commandlist: Lists comprehensive list of commands
-?forcesend: Sends the next queued announcment immeadiatly and removes from queue
-?halt: Pauses the announcment queue
-?continue: Continues the announcment queue
-?clear: Clears the queue
